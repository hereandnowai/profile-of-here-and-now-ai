Stage 1: Empathize

Research Your Users' Needs

Move beyond assumptions and gain genuine understanding of the user. Put yourself in their shoes to comprehend their experiences, motivations, and emotional context.

  • Empathy Interviews - Open-ended questions to elicit rich insights
  • Empathy Mapping - Seeing, Hearing, Saying & Doing, Thinking & Feeling
  • Observation - Watch users in their natural environment
  • Immersion - Experience the problem firsthand
‚ù§Ô∏è
Empathize
www.hereandnowai.com

Stage 2: Define

State Users' Needs and Problems

Synthesize insights from empathy phase into clear, actionable problem statements. Shift from understanding to articulating the challenge.

  • Point of View (POV) - "[User] needs [need] because [insight]"
  • How Might We (HMW) - Reframe challenges as opportunities
  • Problem Statement - Human-centered problem definition
  • User Personas - Keep efforts focused on real users
üéØ
Define
www.hereandnowai.com

Stage 3: Ideate

Challenge Assumptions & Create Ideas

Generate novel solutions through visual thinking. Create a "safe space" where idea quality matters, not artistic ability. Think outside the box!

  • Brainstorming - Quantity over quality initially
  • Sketching - Visual thinking, simple diagrams
  • Worst Possible Idea - Free thinking to expand solution space
  • SCAMPER - Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse
üí°
Ideate
www.hereandnowai.com

Stage 4: Prototype

Start to Create Solutions

Make ideas tangible through "learning by doing". Create preliminary versions that can be tested and experienced. Fail fast, learn faster!

  • Paper Prototypes - Quick, low-cost concept validation
  • Storyboarding - Map the user journey visually
  • Role-Playing - Act out service experiences
  • Digital Mockups - Interactive clickable prototypes
üîß
Prototype
www.hereandnowai.com

Stage 5: Test

Try Your Solutions Out

Get structured feedback from fresh perspectives. Ensure feedback is unbiased and reflects first-time user experience.
